Oracle

Oracle was rescued from a roadside attraction where she lived in a small cage. Since arriving at Wild Oasis, she's become one of the most curious and vocal wolves in the pack. She loves lounging in the shade and howling at sunset.

Chaos

Chaos was found injured and alone in the wild after being separated from his pack. Now healed, he’s a calm and wise presence among the other wolves, often seen watching over them from his favorite hill.

Shasta

Shasta was surrendered by a private owner who realized wolves don’t belong in backyards. She has since embraced the sanctuary life, making fast friends with Oracle and learning how to be part of a real pack.

Little Brother

Little Brother was born in captivity but never knew a safe or loving home until he came to Wild Oasis. He’s playful, full of energy, and loves splashing in the pond on hot days.
